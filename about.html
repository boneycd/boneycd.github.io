About Me

Boney, a dedicated Helpdesk Officer at Pawsey Supercomputing Services, boasts an impressive 10-year track record in tech support and IT administration roles. Armed with a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics & Communication Engineering and multiple certifications in Information Technology, she brings a wealth of expertise to her work. Boney’s professional journey has taken her across various countries, enriching her perspective and broadening her horizons. She recently embarked on the She Codes Plus program, driven by a desire to expand her technical abilities and enhance her performance in her current role. Her long-term vision involves transitioning into a more tech-focused position. The past 6 months have been exhilarating for Boney as she connected with a vibrant community of like-minded women, surpassing her own expectations in learning. She eagerly looks forward to continuing her tech education. Beyond work and coding, Boney cherishes her role as a mumma to two girls and finds solace in nature, spending quality time with her beloved family.
